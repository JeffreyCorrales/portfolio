About & Resume


As a designer on WL2, I was part a smaller team and contributed to gameplay implementation for nearly every level in the game. I created layouts and was also involved in authoring quests and narrative.

The following three levels are the most memorable to me. I spent a substantial time working on Highpool since it was one of the earlier levels and I was the primary owner of Rail Nomads and Angel Oracle.
