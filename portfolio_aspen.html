Overview


Aspen was a ski resort taken over by a psychopath and his army of gas breathing cultists, which meant it was ripe for showcasing the brutality and absurdness of the wasteland. I created a level that flowed between exterior and interior spaces, and filled it with torture puzzles and grotesque skill interactions.

The player had opportunities to rescue hostages, and these opportunites became memorable encounters where the player could fail if they didn't think carefully about their decisions and make use of their character skill sets.

While the rest of the game proceeded at the normal pace, I was tasked with taking Aspen to a polished state for use as a demo. The resulting demo of Aspen was featured at Gamescom in Germany, and it won recognition as "Best Role Playing Game" at the 2019 event.
