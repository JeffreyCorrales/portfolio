Hello!
I’m a game designer and leader who is very fortunate to have had a long game industry career. Over the past two decades, I’ve been able to work on a wide variety of game types, platforms, engines, and technologies.

While I’ve been focused on game and level design for over a decade, I’ve had many roles over the years that have helped me learn a great deal about making games. My journey started as a content developer and I worked my way “up the stack” to gameplay programmer, then engineer, then level designer.

I’ve worked with several different teams, made many friends, and learned several different game production processes along the way.


Why is all of this important?
As a generalist, I’m quick to learn, adapt, and leverage all of that experience to mold whatever we are making into something fantastic. My varied experiences also help me see the bigger picture, see how things can be put together, and often anticipate roadblocks before we hit them so we can course correct in time.

This wide variety of skills and experiences also help make me a better leader and mentor. I’ve been a discipline lead as well as a game director multiple times and received great feedback from my peers. I’ve also mentored other designers and helped guide several to fulfilling career goals. I strive to be empathetic and serve those I lead to help us all achieve greatness.

I love learning new things, growing, and pouring my passion into making games. I hope to keep doing it for many years to come.
