Overview


The Bizarre was a marketplace of the expensive, the dangerous, and the weird, built into a prewar mall buried deep under the snow. It was run by a gang called the Monster Army who were under threat of being overrun by a rival gang of killer clowns called The Payasos.

The idea of a city buried in snow allowed me to create interesting layout connections wherein the player go enter a partially submerged building and head down into the burried depths of the city, an area known as The Warrens, and eventually return to the surface via multiple alternate exits.

I implemented several memorable encounters in The Bizzare. I scripted a sentient vending machine that could give the player grenades for a quarter. Quarters could only be found in the Warrens by lock picking old parking meters, however that meant the player had to deal with patrolling clowns and their "Hot Hog" pigs strapped with dynamite.

Deep in the Warrens the player could fix a defrosting mechanism to rescue a strange man trapped inside a frozen storage unit. The man, named The Provost, would proceed to follow the player for the rest of the game, speaking only in Latin, and providing a payoff in the form of a special sidequest if kept alive until much later in the game.
