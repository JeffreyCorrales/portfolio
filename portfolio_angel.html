Overview


Angel Oracle is what happens when a cult of obnoxiously polite cannibals, called the Mannerites, takes over the LA Coliseum. I implemented a scene wherin a prisoner escapes from the Mannerites and as a result the player is forced to leave a party memeber behind as collatoral until they can venture out and capture the escapee. This was a bespoke scenario in which I worked closely with engineers to find a solution for seralizing the party member data such that it was compatible with our save system.

After returning the escaped prisoner, the player took part of a public exectution in which they could intervene or allow an upset rival faction to overthrow the Mannerites. I scripted a gruesome execution involving two forklifts mounted with chainsaws that eviscerated the poor prisoner into gibs.

The layout for Angel Oracle presented an interesting nav mesh challenge. The stadium was a single large mesh which caused poor navmesh generation along the walkable path. Being handy with 3dsMax I was able to help parition the mesh into sections based on their traversability. Throughout the project and beyond I was heavily relied upon for solving nav mesh issues.
